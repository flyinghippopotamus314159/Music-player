There are ten different beats, each represented by a number 0-9:

0 Silence
1 Bass Drum
2 Crash Cymbal
3 Closed Hi-Hat
4 Open Hi-Hat
5 Sidestick 
6 Snare
7 Tom
8 Ride Cymbal
9 Rim


These digits are simply typed in order of playing.
